# FORGE — Your Training. Your Progress. Your Records.

> The Forge Athlete App gives you everything you need to track your workouts, measure your progress, and see how far you've come — all from your phone.

---

## What Is the Forge Athlete App?

The Forge Athlete App is the training companion for members of The Forge Functional Fitness. It connects directly to your coach's programming, so you always know what's coming, and gives you powerful tools to log your results, track your personal records, and share your achievements.

No more forgotten scores. No more "what did I get last time?" Just open the app, and your entire training history is there.

---

## What You Get

### See Your Workouts

- **Today's workout, ready and waiting** — as soon as your coach publishes the WOD, it appears in your app, formatted exactly as it's written on the whiteboard
- **Filter by class type** — only see workouts relevant to you (WOD, Foundations, Kids & Teens)
- **Coach notes** — read the intended stimulus and scaling guidance before you even walk through the door

### Log Everything

- **Your personal logbook** — after every session, log your results: time, reps, weight, rounds, calories, metres, and scaling level
- **Section-by-section logging** — log each part of the workout separately (Strength result, WOD result, Skills notes) so nothing gets lost
- **Coach-defined scoring** — if your coach sets up custom metrics for a workout, they appear automatically in your log
- **Whiteboard photos** — snap a quick photo of the whiteboard and attach it to that day's entry
- **Browse your history** — view your logbook by day, week, or month. Scroll back through weeks and months of training to see your consistency

### Track Your Benchmarks

- **16 standard CrossFit benchmarks** — Fran, Grace, Murph, Filthy Fifty, and more. Log your result each time you test, and watch your progress on a chart over time
- **Forge benchmarks** — your gym's own signature workouts, tracked the same way as the classics. These are the workouts that define your community
- **Scaling levels** — log whether you went RX or Scaled, so your progress chart tells the full story

### Track Your Lifts

- **All major barbell movements** — Back Squat, Front Squat, Deadlift, Bench Press, Overhead Press, Snatch, Clean & Jerk, and more
- **Multiple rep schemes** — track your 1RM, 3RM, 5RM, and 10RM separately for each lift
- **Automatic 1RM calculator** — enter any weight and rep count, and the app estimates your one-rep max. Useful when you hit a heavy set of 3 and want to know where your 1RM sits
- **PR detection** — the app automatically spots when you've set a new personal record and highlights it

### Your Personal Records

- **Records gallery** — all your PRs across benchmarks and lifts, displayed in one place
- **Shareable cards** — generate a branded image of your PR with your photo, the workout details, and the gym logo. Perfect for posting to Instagram, WhatsApp, or your gym's group chat
- **Track your journey** — see at a glance how much you've improved since you started

### Community & Competition

- **Workout leaderboards** — after each WOD, see how your result compares to other athletes at the gym. Ranked by time, reps, or weight depending on the workout
- **Fist bumps** — tap to give encouragement on any athlete's logged result. A simple way to recognise effort, celebrate PRs, and build community
- **Fair ranking** — scaling levels are separated, ties are broken properly, and the leaderboard reflects genuine performance

### Achievements

- **Earn badges** — your coach defines achievements across categories like Strength, Gymnastics, Endurance, and Consistency, with tier levels from Bronze to Gold
- **Claim your own** — hit a milestone? Claim the achievement yourself and your coach will verify it
- **Build your collection** — your earned badges appear on your profile for everyone to see

### Workout Timer

- **5 built-in timer modes** — AMRAP, EMOM, Tabata, For Time, and Interval
- **Fullscreen mode** — prop your phone up and use it as your personal timer during a workout
- **No extra app needed** — the timer is built right in, ready when you need it

### Exercise Video Library

- **Form reference at your fingertips** — exercises in the library include video links so you can check proper form before or during a session

### Notifications

- **Stay in the loop** — receive push notifications when:
  - A new workout is published
  - You set a new personal record
  - Someone fist-bumps your result
  - You earn an achievement
- **You're in control** — choose exactly which notifications you want. Turn off what you don't need

### Family Accounts

- **One login for the whole family** — if your spouse or children also train at The Forge, they're linked under your account
- **Separate profiles** — each family member has their own logbook, benchmarks, lifts, and records
- **Easy switching** — toggle between profiles with a tap at the top of the screen
- **Book for your kids** — parents can manage class bookings for the whole family

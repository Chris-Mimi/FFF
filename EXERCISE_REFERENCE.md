## 📋 Quick Reference

### Format Rules

✅ **Correct formats:**
- `10 x Burpee` (singular)
- `25 Push-up Strict` (exact display name)
- `50 Forward Lunge` (use specific variant)
- `100 AbMat Sit-Up` (exact display name)
- `* Airsquat` (bullets work)

❌ **Won't match:**
- `10 x Burpees` (plural)
- `25 push-ups` (plural, but case-insensitive is OK)
- `50 Lunges` (use specific type: Forward Lunge, Walking Lunge, etc.)
